EMPATHIC MODELING

Empathic modelling is a research method whereby an individual adopts different tools, wearables or software simulators, to simulate the (dis)abilities of users in everyday scenarios. (Torrens 2011)

Through empathic modeling, the designer aligns with user's felt experience and values, which are critical for defining the basis for the usability and appropriateness of designed artifacts. (Nicolle 2003)
